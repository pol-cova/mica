# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community. Participation must be free from harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and abusive conduct.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, assume good intent, respect differing viewpoints, protect sensitive operational information, and give feedback about ideas rather than people.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, hate speech, sexualized language, doxxing, sharing private credentials or customer data, and deliberately unsafe operational guidance are not acceptable.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the project maintainer through the repository’s security contact. Reports will be handled promptly and with discretion.
